# -

Performs a subtraction operation, or serves as an negative sign. 

**Syntax** |  **Description**  
---|---  
y = x - z;  |  Subtract z from x.   
y = -x;  |  Negative   
  
**Examples**

This example shows the usage of the "-" operator. 
    
    
    x=1:3;
    y=2:4;
    ?z=x-y;
    result: 
    -1  
    -1  
    -1
