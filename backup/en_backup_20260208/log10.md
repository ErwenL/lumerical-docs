# log10

Calculates the base 10 logarithm function. Input can be complex or negative. 

**Syntax** |  **Description**  
---|---  
out = log10(x);  |  The base 10 logarithm of x. Input can be complex or negative.   
  
**Example**

Calculate the base 10 logarithm of a number. 
    
    
    ?log10(100);
     result: 
     2
