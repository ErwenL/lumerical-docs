# sin

Calculates the trigonometric sine function. Angle units are in radians. The function is defined for complex angles. Phase of a complex number is evaluated between -  π  and  π  . 

**Syntax** |  **Description**  
---|---  
out = sin(x);  |  Returns the complex sine of x.   
  
**Example**

Calculate sin(  π  /4 + i). 
    
    
    theta=pi/4+1i;
    ?sin(theta);
    result: 
    1.09112+0.830993i
