# ceil

Rounds the input to the nearest integer greater than or equal to itself. 

**Syntax** |  **Description**  
---|---  
out = ceil(x);  |  Returns the nearest integer greater than or equal to x.   
  
**Example**

Find the nearest integer greater than or equal to 1.41: 
    
    
    ?ceil(1.41);
    result:
    2
