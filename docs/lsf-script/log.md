# log

Calculates the natural logarithm function. Input can be complex or negative. 

**Syntax** |  **Description**  
---|---  
out = log(x);  |  The natural logarithm of x. Input can be complex or negative.   
  
**Example**

Check that the natural logarithm of e is 1. 
    
    
    ?Exp=exp(1);
    result: 
    2.71828 
    ?log(Exp); # Result should be 1
    result: 
    1 Â
