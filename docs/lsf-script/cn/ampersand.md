# ampersand

**语法** | **描述**
---|---
out = y & x; | If the 实部 part of either or both of x,y are 零, then 返回 0. Otherwise 返回 1.
y and x; | Same as &.

**示例**

This 示例 shows the usage of the "&" and AND 函数. 
    ? (2) & (4);
    result:
    1
    ?(3  4) and (4 3);
    result: 
    0 
    ? (0+1i) & (1);
    result:
    0

    result:
    1
    ?(3  4) and (4 3);
    result: 
    0 
    ? (0+1i) & (1);
    result:
    0
